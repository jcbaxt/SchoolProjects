Welcome to the University of Louisville Commercialization EPI-Center

Welcome to the new University of Louisville Commercialization EPI-Center, which takes the ground-breaking discoveries created by innovators across UofL from mind to market. Our new office name describes the center of our focus: Commercialization, Entrepreneurship, Partnerships and Innovation. As UofL’s “epicenter” for commercialization, we will build on our past successes to seek and harness new resources aimed at helping our UofL innovators succeed.

Allen Morris, Director, Commercialization EPI-Center
